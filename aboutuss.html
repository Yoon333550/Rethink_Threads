About Us

We are Year 7 Fuchsia Class 1, Group 5 students working on a PBL project about Textile Waste Awareness. Our project focuses on reducing textile waste and encouraging people to live in cleaner, greener, and smarter cities.


Through this project, we researched how fast fashion affects the environment and learned about sustainable solutions that can help reduce pollution and waste. We created this website to spread awareness and inspire others to make eco-friendly choices in their daily lives.

Our Goal

We want to help create cleaner, greener, and smarter communities for the future.

Our project encourages people to recycle old clothes, donate unused items, reuse materials, and reduce textile waste.

We believe even small eco-friendly actions can make a big difference in protecting the environment.

This project also helped us improve our teamwork, creativity, coding, communication, and research skills.
