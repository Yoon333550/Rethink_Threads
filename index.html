Textile Waste

Our PBL project focuses on building sustainable smart cities and raising awareness about zero waste. We looked at how pollution, fast fashion, and textile waste impact both communities and the environment.


Through research and teamwork, we discovered that even small eco‑friendly actions can make a big difference. By changing habits and finding creative solutions, we can help create cleaner, greener, and smarter cities for the future.

Impact

Fast fashion leads to heavy pollution and creates massive amounts of textile waste in cities worldwide. This affects communities, harms the environment, and adds to the challenges of building sustainable smart cities.

Solution

We promote sustainable smart city ideas by encouraging eco‑friendly habits, upcycling, and creative ways to reduce waste. Small actions, when combined, can help build cleaner and greener communities

Why Sustainable Smart Cities Matter

Sustainable smart cities are designed to reduce pollution, save resources, and create healthier environments for both people and nature. Our project highlights the importance of reducing waste, recycling materials, donating clothes, and making better environmental choices. Even the smallest actions can add up, creating a lasting impact and helping future generations live in cleaner, greener, and smarter communities.
